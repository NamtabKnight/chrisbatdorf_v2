Why Me?

When you choose to work with me for your next project, I bring a lot to the table. I will not even use a majority of the tools, because honestly, the majority of projects do not require utilizing every trick and tool under the sun. In my experience, when a project tries to do too much, the overall quality of the project suffers. This means your end user suffers, which then means your business suffers as well. I promise to give an honest assessment, deliver a clean, manageable solution, and produce a solution that will help you to thrive.
